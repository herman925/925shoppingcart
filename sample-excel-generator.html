This tool generates a sample Excel database file (MasterDatabase.xlsx) with authentic Tai Po Hong Kong stores and typical household items that a housewife would buy. The file includes accurate English, Traditional Chinese, and Indonesian translations.

  1. B - Item_EN: Item name in English

  4. E - Category: Item category (Food, Health, Household, etc.)
  5. F - Store_EN: Store name in English
  6. G - Store_CN: Store name in Traditional Chinese
  7. H - Store_ID: Store name in Indonesian
  8. I - Address_EN: Store address in English
  9. J - Address_CN: Store address in Traditional Chinese
  10. K - Address_ID: Store address in Indonesian
